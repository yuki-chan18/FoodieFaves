Fried Bangus Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole bangus (milkfish), cleaned and butterflied
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil (for frying)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix vinegar, garlic, salt, black pepper, and soy sauce.
  2. Marinate the bangus in the mixture for at least 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Fry the bangus skin side down first until crispy and golden brown.
  5. Flip and fry the other side until cooked through.
  6. Drain excess oil on paper towels.
  7. Serve hot with rice and dipping sauce. Enjoy!
