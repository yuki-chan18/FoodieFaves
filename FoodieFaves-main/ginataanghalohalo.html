Ginataang Halo-Halo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup taro (gabi), peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup jackfruit, sliced
  • 1/4 cup saba bananas, sliced
  • 1/4 cup tapioca pearls (sago), cooked
  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine coconut milk, coconut cream, and a pinch of salt. Bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced sweet potato and taro. Cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until the root vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the jackfruit, saba bananas, and cooked tapioca pearls. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Continue cooking for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. Stir in the sugar and adjust sweetness to taste. Add vanilla extract for extra flavor if desired.
  6. If using, add the sweetened red beans and mix well.
  7. Once everything is heated through and the mixture has thickened slightly, remove from heat.
  8. Serve warm or chilled in bowls. Enjoy your Ginataang Halo-Halo!
