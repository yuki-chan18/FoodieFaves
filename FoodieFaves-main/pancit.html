Pancit Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pancit bihon (rice noodles)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1/2 lb chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/2 cup shrimp (optional)
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 cup green beans, sliced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 lemon, sliced (for garnish)
  • Chopped green onions (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water for 10 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned. If using shrimp, add them and cook until pink.
  4. Add cabbage, carrots, and green beans. Stir-fry for a few minutes.
  5. Pour in chicken broth, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Add the soaked noodles and toss until well coated in sauce.
  7. Cook until noodles absorb the liquid and are tender.
  8. Season with black pepper and mix well.
  9. Garnish with green onions and lemon slices before serving. Enjoy!
