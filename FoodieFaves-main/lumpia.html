Lumpia Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 25 lumpia wrappers
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix ground pork, carrots, cabbage, garlic, onion, egg, soy sauce, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Lay a lumpia wrapper on a flat surface and place a spoonful of filling near one edge.
  3. Roll tightly, folding in the sides, and seal with a dab of water.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and fry lumpia until golden brown.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with sweet chili sauce. Enjoy!
