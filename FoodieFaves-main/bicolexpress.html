Bicol Express Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork belly, sliced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 5-6 red chili peppers (siling labuyo), sliced
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the pork belly and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in shrimp paste and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and water. Let it simmer for 20 minutes until the pork is tender.
  5. Add chili peppers, salt, and black pepper. Stir well.
  6. Pour in coconut cream and continue to cook until the sauce thickens.
  7. Adjust seasoning as needed. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
