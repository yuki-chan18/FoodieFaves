Adobong Pusit Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb squid (pusit), cleaned and cut into rings
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the squid and cook for about 2-3 minutes until it turns opaque.
  3. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, water, bay leaves, black pepper, and sugar. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring to a boil and then lower the heat. Let it simmer for about 5-7 minutes until the squid is tender and the sauce has slightly reduced.
  5. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
