Menudo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork, cubed
  • 1/2 lb pork liver, cubed
  • 1 small potato, diced
  • 1 small carrot, diced
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 small red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water or broth
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the pork and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in soy sauce and tomato sauce. Stir and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add water or broth, then bring to a simmer. Cover and cook until the pork is tender.
  5. Add the pork liver and mix well.
  6. Stir in the potatoes, carrots, green peas, and red bell pepper. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Simmer for a few more minutes until the sauce thickens.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
