Pochero Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork belly or beef, cubed
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 pieces plantain bananas (saba), sliced
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 small cabbage, chopped
  • 1 bunch bok choy (pechay)
  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pot and saut√© garlic, onions, and tomatoes until soft.
  2. Add the pork or beef and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in water and bring to a boil. Simmer until meat is tender.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, fish sauce, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Add the potatoes, plantain bananas, and chickpeas. Cook until tender.
  6. Add the cabbage and bok choy. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
