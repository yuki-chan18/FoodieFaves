Laing Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dried taro leaves
  • 1/2 lb pork, sliced
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 2 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 5-6 pieces red chili peppers, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the pork and cook until slightly browned.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Stir in the shrimp paste and chili peppers.
  5. Gently add the dried taro leaves, making sure not to stir too much.
  6. Let it simmer until the taro leaves are tender and fully absorb the flavors.
  7. Pour in the coconut cream and continue cooking until the sauce thickens.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
