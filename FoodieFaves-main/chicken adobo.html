Chicken Adobo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until slightly browned.
  3. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, and water.
  4. Add bay leaves and black peppercorns.
  5. Simmer for 30-40 minutes until chicken is tender.
  6. Optionally, add sugar to balance flavors.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
