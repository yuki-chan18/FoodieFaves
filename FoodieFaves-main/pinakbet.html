Pinakbet Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork belly, sliced into cubes
  • 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined (optional)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium eggplant, sliced
  • 1 cup bitter melon (ampalaya), sliced thinly
  • 1 cup squash, cubed
  • 1/2 cup string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup okra, sliced
  • 2 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat a little oil over medium heat and saut√© garlic, onions, and tomatoes until softened.
  2. Add pork belly cubes and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Once the pork is browned, add the shrimp paste (bagoong) and mix well, allowing it to cook for a minute or two.
  4. Add the water and bring to a boil. Let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes until the pork is tender.
  5. Once the pork is tender, add the squash, eggplant, string beans, okra, and bitter melon. Stir well to combine.
  6. Let the vegetables cook for another 10-15 minutes until tender. Stir occasionally and add more water if necessary.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Once the vegetables are tender and the flavors are well combined, remove from heat.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
