Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs pork ribs or belly, cut into chunks
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 packet tamarind soup base mix
  • 1 daikon radish, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 10 string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach)
  • 2-3 green chili peppers
  • Salt and fish sauce to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add pork, onion, and tomatoes.
  2. Simmer for 30-40 minutes until pork is tender.
  3. Add the tamarind soup base mix and stir well.
  4. Add radish, eggplant, and string beans. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Add kangkong and green chili peppers, then simmer for 2 more minutes.
  6. Season with salt and fish sauce to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
