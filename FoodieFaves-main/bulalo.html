Bulalo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef shank with bone marrow
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 1 head of garlic, smashed
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 2 corn on the cob, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 bunch bok choy (pechay)
  • 1 medium cabbage, quartered
  • 1-2 long green chili peppers (optional)
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce (patis)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 10 cups water

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine beef shank, onion, garlic, and water. Bring to a boil.
  2. Once it starts boiling, lower the heat and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours or until the beef becomes tender. Skim off any scum that rises to the surface.
  3. Add the fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Continue simmering for another 15 minutes.
  4. Add the potatoes and corn and cook for another 10 minutes or until tender.
  5. Add the bok choy and cabbage, then cook for another 5-10 minutes, just until the vegetables are tender but still vibrant.
  6. If you want some heat, add the long green chili peppers.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
