Tapsilog Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin or tenderloin, sliced thinly
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp oil (for frying the eggs)
  • 1/2 cup atchara (pickled papaya) (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, minced garlic, brown sugar, black pepper, and salt. Marinate the beef in the mixture for at least 30 minutes or overnight for better flavor.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Saut√© the marinated beef until it is cooked through and browned on all sides. Set aside.
  3. In a separate pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil and fry the eggs sunny side up or to your desired level of doneness.
  4. To assemble, place a serving of rice on a plate. Top with the cooked beef and a fried egg.
  5. Serve with atchara (pickled papaya) on the side, if desired.
  6. Enjoy your tapsilog with a hot cup of coffee or your preferred drink!
