Kare-Kare Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb oxtail, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 lb tripe, cleaned and sliced (optional)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp annatto powder (achuete)
  • 1/2 cup ground toasted rice
  • 1/2 lb banana heart, sliced
  • 1/2 lb eggplant, sliced
  • 1/2 lb string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1/2 lb bok choy
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bagoong (shrimp paste) for serving

Instructions:

  1. Boil the oxtail and tripe in water until tender. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add the boiled oxtail and tripe. Stir well.
  4. Dissolve annatto powder in a little water and add to the pan.
  5. Mix in peanut butter and ground toasted rice, stirring until sauce thickens.
  6. Add the vegetables and let them cook until tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with bagoong (shrimp paste) on the side. Enjoy!
