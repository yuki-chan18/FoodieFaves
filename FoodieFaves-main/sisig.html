Sisig Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork belly
  • 1/2 lb pork liver
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 egg (optional, for topping)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Chopped green chilies (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Grill or pan-fry the pork belly and liver until fully cooked and slightly crispy.
  2. Chop the pork belly and liver into small pieces.
  3. In a pan, melt butter and saut√© garlic and onions until fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped pork and liver, then season with soy sauce, calamansi juice, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Mix in mayonnaise and chili flakes for added flavor.
  6. Transfer to a sizzling plate and top with a raw egg, allowing the heat to cook it.
  7. Garnish with green chilies and serve hot. Enjoy!
