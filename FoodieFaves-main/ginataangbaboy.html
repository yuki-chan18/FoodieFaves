Ginataang Baboy Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork belly, cubed
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1-2 small chilies (optional, for spice)
  • 1 bunch string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 1 cup eggplant, cubed
  • 1/2 cup malunggay leaves or spinach (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add pork belly and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in fish sauce, soy sauce, black pepper, and water. Let it simmer until pork is tender.
  4. Once the pork is tender, add coconut milk, red bell pepper, and optional chilies. Stir well.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the coconut milk to thicken.
  6. Add the string beans and eggplant. Cook until vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  7. Finally, stir in malunggay leaves or spinach, and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
